The **Premium Management** page displays information about your server's premium subscription.

If you are the customer who purchased the subscription, a link to manage your subscription on [Paddle](https://paddle.com) will be provided.

<Aside type="note">
  If you are not the customer who purchased the subscription, you will only see
  the subscription status and next payment/expiration date.
</Aside>
