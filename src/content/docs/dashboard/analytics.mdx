The analytics page provides insights into the server's tickets and and feedback.

## Ticket Analytics

The ticket analytics section displays a chart showing the number of tickets along with some key metrics.

## Feedback Analytics

The feedback analytics section displays a chart showing the average feedback rating along with some key metrics.

Here you can see the average rating, the total number of feedback entries, and the distribution of ratings from 1 to 5 stars, as well as the relations between ratings and staff.
You can filter the analytics by a specific time range using the date picker at the top right of the page.

<Aside type="note">
  For the feedback-staff relation, staff must claim tickets for their feedback
  to be counted towards them.
</Aside>
